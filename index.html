Physiotherapy Services

Our expert physiotherapists provide top-tier, personalized care in Dubai, helping you recover from injuries and improve mobility

Cardio respiratory physiotherapy

Cardiorespiratory physical therapy helps patients with respiratory and cardiovascular conditions improve physical function and health. It is essential for managing diseases like COPD, asthma, heart failure, and recovery from heart surgeries or lung transplants

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Orthopedic rehabilitation is a specialized physical therapy aimed at helping patients recover from musculoskeletal injuries and conditions. It is essential for those recovering from surgeries, trauma, or chronic issues involving bones, joints, ligaments, and muscles

Geriatric physiotherapy

Geriatric physiotherapy focuses on the unique physical needs of older adults, addressing age-related conditions and promoting mobility. It aims to enhance strength, balance, and overall quality of life through tailored interventions and support for maintaining independence

Sports Rehabilitation

Sports rehabilitation focuses on helping athletes recover from injuries and enhance their physical performance. It combines assessment, tailored exercise programs, and injury prevention strategies to facilitate a safe return to sports

Comprehensive Rehabilitation

Comprehensive rehabilitation provides holistic care to individuals recovering from illness or injury, addressing physical, emotional, and social needs. It involves a multidisciplinary approach to enhance functional abilities and improve overall quality of life

Pain management

Pain management focuses on alleviating discomfort and improving quality of life for individuals with acute or chronic pain. It employs various techniques, including medication, physical therapy, and psychological support, tailored to each patient's needs.

Electrotherapy

Electrotherapy uses electrical modalities to treat pain, promote healing, and improve muscle function. It includes techniques such as TENS, ultrasound, and electrical stimulation to enhance rehabilitation outcomes

Neuro Rehabilitation

Neuro rehabilitation focuses on helping individuals recover from neurological conditions, such as strokes, traumatic brain injuries, and spinal cord injuries. It employs specialized therapies to improve movement, function, and quality of life through personalized treatment plans

Therapeutic exercises

Therapeutic exercises are designed to improve strength, flexibility, and overall function for individuals recovering from injuries or managing chronic conditions. These exercises are tailored to each patient's needs, promoting optimal recovery and enhancing physical performance

Kinesio Taping Techniques

Kinesio taping techniques involve applying elastic therapeutic tape to support and stabilize muscles and joints without restricting movement. This method helps reduce pain and inflammation while enhancing circulation and promoting recovery during rehabilitation

Pediatric physiotherapy

Pediatric physiotherapy focuses on the physical development and rehabilitation of infants, children, and adolescents with various conditions. It uses play-based and tailored interventions to enhance mobility, strength, and coordination, supporting overall growth and function

Antenatal And Postnatal Physiotherapy

Antenatal and postnatal physiotherapy provides specialized care for women during and after pregnancy to address physical changes and challenges. It focuses on promoting comfort, managing pain, and enhancing recovery, ensuring overall well-being for mothers and their newborns

Post operative management

Post-operative management involves the care and rehabilitation of patients after surgery to ensure smooth recovery and prevent complications. Tailored interventions are essential for restoring function and promoting optimal healing

Manual therapy techniques

Manual therapy techniques are hands-on approaches that alleviate pain, improve mobility, and promote healing. This method includes joint manipulation, soft tissue mobilization, and stretching to relieve muscle tension and enhance joint function. Through gentle pressure, manual therapy helps break down scar tissue and restore natural movement, making it effective for chronic pain, stiffness, and recovery.

1

Joint mobilization

Joint mobilization gently restores natural movement in stiff or restricted joints.

2

Myofascial release

Myofascial release relieves tension and improves flexibility in restricted muscles.

3

Muscle Energy Techniques

Muscle energy techniques use controlled contractions to improve mobility and reduce stiffness

4

Soft tissue mobilization

Soft tissue mobilization effectively relieves muscle tension and improves function

5

Trigger point therapy

Trigger point therapy effectively relieves deep muscle pain and improves joint function.

6

Joint manipulation

Joint manipulation quickly restores normal joint movement and effectively relieves pain

7

Stretching

Stretching improves flexibility, muscle function, and reduces injury risk by increasing joint mobility.

8

Manual Traction

Manual traction relieves pressure, improves mobility, and reduces pain by gently stretching the spine.

9

Strain Counterstrain

Strain Counterstrain alleviates pain and restores mobility by gently positioning muscles to release tension.

10

Active assistance range of motion

Active assistance range of motion improves joint flexibility and muscle strength through voluntary movement with support.

11

Active Release techniques

Active Release Techniques alleviate pain and improve mobility by targeting and releasing tight muscles and fascia through specific movements.

12

Dry Needling

Dry needling is a therapy that uses thin needles to relieve muscle pain, tension, and improve mobility.
